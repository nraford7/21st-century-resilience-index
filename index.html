21st Century Resilience Index

A compound measure of national resilience to oil shocks and readiness for 21st century energy, economic, and technological transitions.

Four Dimensions

Clean Energy Share

% of primary energy from renewables + nuclear. Measures structural insulation from fossil fuel price volatility.

Purchasing Power

GDP per capita at PPP (log-normalized). Economic capacity to absorb shocks and invest in transition.

Clean Tech Production

Net production and export of solar, batteries, EVs, wind turbines. Industrial position in the transition economy.

Compute & Automation

Data centers, semiconductors, supercomputing, AI infrastructure. Capacity for the automation economy.
